General Health Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drinking an adequate amount of water daily is crucial for your overall health. It helps in maintaining bodily functions, regulating temperature, and transporting nutrients.

Eat a Balanced Diet

A diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains provides your body with the essential vitamins and minerals it needs to thrive.

Exercise Regularly

Regular physical activity strengthens your heart, improves your mood, and helps you maintain a healthy weight. Aim for at least 30 minutes a day.
